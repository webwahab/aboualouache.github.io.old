    Short Biography

    I received engineer, Magistre, and Ph.D. degrees in computer science from University of Sciences and Technology HOUARI BOUMEDIENE (USTHB), Algeria, in 2010, 2013 and 2016 respectively. I have 7 years of experience both in Industry and Academia. I am the leader of two research projects and was involved in a set of important projects in mobile and internet companies. I have published a set of scientific papers in reputed scientific journals and conferences. I was on internship at the DRIVE Lab of the University of Burgundy, France, from 2015 to 2016. I have been serving as a TPC member of different conferences and workshops such as ICC, GLOBECOM, Wireless Days, and ISC2. The innovation, the creatively, and the project management are my main personal qualities. His research interest includes security and privacy in Vehicular Networks, and internet of things.
